Effective Date: March 6, 2026

🔒 Zero-Egress Guarantee: The @passiveintent/core SDK runs entirely on the client device and makes zero network requests to our servers. Your end-users' behavioral data never leaves their browser.

1. Introduction

Welcome to PassiveIntent ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by PassiveIntent Technologies LLP. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit passiveintent.dev (the "Website") or purchase our software licenses.

2. The PassiveIntent Engine (Zero-Egress Guarantee)

PassiveIntent is engineered as a privacy-first, zero-egress behavioral engine. If you deploy the @passiveintent/core SDK on your application, we do not collect, receive, or process any of your end-users' data. The software executes entirely within the local client environment and makes zero network requests to our servers.

3. Information We Collect from Website Visitors & Customers

While our software engine collects no data, we do collect limited information when you interact with our Website or purchase a license:

  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and communication history.
  • Billing Information: When you purchase a commercial license, your payment is processed securely by our Merchant of Record (e.g., Lemon Squeezy). We do not collect or store your raw credit card details. We only receive your name, email, company name, and billing address for license generation and tax compliance.
  • Website Analytics: We may collect basic, anonymized telemetry regarding website traffic to improve our developer documentation and marketing.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver your license keys and commercial software access.
  • To provide technical support and customer service.
  • To comply with legal and tax obligations.

5. Third-Party Services

We use a Merchant of Record (Lemon Squeezy / Paddle) to handle all global tax compliance, invoicing, and payment processing. By purchasing a license, your billing data is subject to their respective privacy policies.
