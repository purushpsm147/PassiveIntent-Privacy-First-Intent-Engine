Bring Your Own Baseline

Calibrated to Your Topography, Not a Global Average.

Every website has a unique “Perfect Buyer” path. PassiveIntent doesn’t use hardcoded thresholds. You inject your own historical analytics data to pre-train the engine’s Markov baseline — and the statistical model adapts its Z-score variance to match your traffic profile automatically.

High-Variance Sites

Media & Editorial

Exploratory browsing is the norm, not an anomaly. The engine mathematically relaxes its Z-score thresholds to accommodate natural content discovery — so you only fire interventions when a session is a genuine statistical outlier against your own traffic, not a generic industry benchmark.

Strict-Funnel Sites

SaaS & Checkout

Every navigation step is intentional. The engine tightens its variance model, detecting hesitation the millisecond a user drifts from the critical path — whether that’s stalling on the billing page or back-navigating from checkout.

Do we need to send behavioral data to your servers?

No. Intent evaluation runs locally in the browser by default.

Do we need a cookie banner for PassiveIntent to work?

No. PassiveIntent itself does not require analytics cookies or server-side behavioral tracking.

No. Product, growth, and CX teams can use signals for practical intervention playbooks.
